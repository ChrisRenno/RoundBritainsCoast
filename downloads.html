Downloads

We don't want you to be badly prepared so we have provided the gpx files that Don painstakingly created for you to download and use on your gps unit. You can also get maps and directions to print out if you would prefer the old-fashioned methods! You'll need to do this from the stages page though. If this goes on to become a permanent audax you'll need a brevet card to attach your receipts to which we have provided.
